What does this website do?

This is a web application that takes your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender outputs your Akan Name.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

Day Female Name Male Name
Sunday Akosua Kwasi
Monday Adwoa Kwadwo
Tuesday Abenaa Kwabena
Wednesday Akua Kwaku
Thursday Yaa Yaw
Friday Afua Kofi
Saturday Ama Kwame
